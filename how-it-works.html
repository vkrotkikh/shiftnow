There are 5 simple steps to take for a quick anonymous exchange at the Best rate!

Enter amount and address

  • Type the amount of funds you want to exchange in “You send” field and chose the currency from a drop-down menu

Check the details and accept terms

  • Review and accept the Terms of Use

Send your funds

Wait for the exchange to complete

  • and complete the exchange
